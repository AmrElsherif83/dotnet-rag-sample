ACME Corporation - Employee Handbook Summary

This is a fictional employee handbook for demonstration purposes only.

Work Schedule:
Standard work hours are Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM. Flexible scheduling is available with manager approval. Core hours (when all team members should be available) are 10 AM to 3 PM.

Remote Work Policy:
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week after completing their 90-day probationary period. Remote work arrangements must be approved by your direct manager. A stable internet connection and appropriate home office setup are required.

Paid Time Off:
- Vacation: 20 days per year (accrued monthly)
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year
- Personal Days: 3 days per year
- Holidays: 10 company holidays per year

Unused vacation days (up to 5) may be carried over to the following year. Sick leave does not carry over.

Benefits Overview:
- Health Insurance: Medical, dental, and vision coverage available
- 401(k): Company matches up to 4% of salary
- Professional Development: $1,500 annual learning budget
- Wellness Program: Gym membership reimbursement up to $50/month

Code of Conduct:
All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior, respect colleagues, and uphold company values of integrity, innovation, and collaboration.

Reporting Issues:
Concerns can be reported to your manager, HR department, or through our anonymous ethics hotline. All reports are kept confidential to the extent possible.

Note: This is sample content for demonstration only. Not real company policy.
